What is this Website?

    Hi, and welcome to my website!
This website is created to give people, such as yourself, the ability to post a review and leave thoughts
on any game that you please! There will also be a list of games that will be provided to you, in case you want something new.
These games have a description, including their genre, and a cover of the game in case you are unfimiliar with it.
You can also check out any reviews that have been posted by other people to see if you share mutual feelings on a particular game!

    REMEMBER:
 Your review is not exclusive to the list of games provided. This website is made for you to freely leave a review on any game that you want.
The list of games is there for you if you want to explore more popular games, read their description, and leave a review based
on what you read.

    IMPORTANT NOTE:
 In order to be able to do all this, you must be logged into the website. To do this, click on
the 'Login' word on the top right, and login in with your details if you have been here before. If you do not have an account,
click on the option to 'Sign Up'. Unless you make an account, you will be restricted to only being able to view reviews.

    After creating an account and/or logging in:
 Once you have done that, you will be able to fully access the website and explore a range of games to review by clicking on the
'Games to Review' button on the top right. By doing this, you would have also gained the ability to leave
your own review, and give your opinion on any game you please, even if its more than one.
